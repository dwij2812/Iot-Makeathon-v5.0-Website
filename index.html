About

Internet of Things (IoT) is one of the rapidly growing technologies that find application in almost all industries. The IoT working committee of the School of Electronics, VIT Chennai, regularly organizes 24-hour IoT Makeathon, to tap the technical skills and the creativity of the students. The novel solutions presented by the participants receive lucrative cash rewards.

VENUE

VIT Chennai

Vandalur Kelambakkam Road

VIT campus is spread across a vast area of 177 acres. A striking feature of VIT is the well- planned and comprehensive infrastructure provided for both students and faculty. Fully furnished and well-equipped labs decorate every building in the University with over 83 servers to manage all the data transmissions. Moreover, the university is blessed with ample features that support all kinds of events - Auditoriums, plush open spaces, outdoor stages and much more.
